Oeufs Mimosa also known as Devilled Eggs are a wonderful appetizer to celebrate the arrival of spring! This version is topped with smoked salmon & capers for a twist on a classic dijon devilled egg.

Ingredients

  • 12 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup quality mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons dijon mustard
  • 11 tablespoon lemon juice, freshly squeezed
  • Salt & pepper, to taste
  • Smoked salmon, to garnish
  • Thinly sliced chives, to garnish
  • Capers, to garnish
  • Smoked flaked salt, to garnish

Step

  1. Fill a large pot with cold water & add the eggs. Bring to a boil & remove from heat. Cover with lid & let stand for 10 minutes. Immediately strain into an ice bath.
  2. Peel the eggs & cut in half to expose the yolk. Transfer the yolks to a small mixing bowl (use a food processor if piping) & transfer the whites to a serving plate.
  3. To the yolks add the mayonnaise, mustard, lemon juice, salt & pepper. Mix until smooth.
  4. Spoon (or pipe) filling back into the egg whites. Top with smoked salmon, chives & a pinch of flaked salt.
